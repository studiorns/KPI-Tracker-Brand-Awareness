Performance Overview

Key Insights

Brand Awareness initiatives have demonstrated exceptional performance through September 2025, with outstanding results across all three sub-initiatives. Brand Awareness T1 achieved 124.1% YTD impressions (13.18B vs 10.62B forecast), 135.9% engagement (2.43B vs 1.79B forecast), and 187.5% paid sessions (45.89M vs 24.48M forecast). Brand Awareness UAE delivered strong results with 133.7% impressions achievement (942M vs 705M forecast), 113.1% engagement (136M vs 120M forecast), and 209.1% paid sessions (2.99M vs 1.43M forecast). Brand Awareness T2 showed exceptional performance with 123.7% impressions (5.88B vs 4.76B forecast), 128.2% engagement (978M vs 763M forecast), and outstanding 218.2% paid sessions achievement (26.0M vs 11.91M forecast). September maintained the strong performance trend with all initiatives continuing to exceed forecasts, demonstrating sustained excellence across all brand awareness KPIs throughout the year.
