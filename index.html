Key Insights

Brand Awareness initiatives have demonstrated exceptional performance through May 2025, surpassing expectations with strong achievement against forecast across all key metrics. Brand Awareness UAE shows mixed performance with strong paid sessions performance (+5.9%) but impressions slightly below forecast (-6.0%). Overall, the portfolio demonstrates robust momentum through the first five months of 2025.
